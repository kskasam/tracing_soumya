How to use:
  1. Paste your SVG path in the "SVG Path Input" field (or load from Telugu letter)
  2. Click "Load SVG" to display the path
  3. Click "Start New Stroke" to begin tracing
  4. Free-hand tracing: Click and drag along the path - points will be recorded continuously
  5. Point-by-point: Or click individual points on the path
  6. Release mouse to pause, click "Finish Current Stroke" when done
  7. Repeat for all strokes
  8. Click "Generate JSON" to get the final JSON output

Tip: You can start tracing from anywhere on the path. Just click and drag in the direction you want to trace!
